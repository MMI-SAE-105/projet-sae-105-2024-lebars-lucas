Miller's Crossing

Directed by the Coen brothers, this film highlights a hat as a central symbol of the protagonist Tom's lost innocence. In this context of betrayal and deceit, the hat becomes an obsession for the main character, illustrating his struggles in the criminal world.

Indiana Jones

The famous archaeologist, portrayed by Harrison Ford, is always seen with his fedora hat. This iconic accessory has become inseparable from his character, symbolizing his adventurous spirit and bravery through perilous quests.

Borsalino

This film stars Alain Delon and Jean-Paul Belmondo, whose very title refers to a type of hat. The Borsalino becomes a symbol of the thug world within the film's context, enhancing the image of elegant gangsters.

Harry Potter

In this series, the Sorting Hat is a character in its own right, determining which house Hogwarts students will belong to. This magical hat plays a crucial role in the characters' identities throughout their school journey.

The Wizard of Oz

The film features the Wicked Witch of the West wearing a large black hat that has become iconic. This hat adds to the menacing aura of the character and contributes to the collective imagination surrounding the film.
