The Hat
The hat is a headgear
used by humans
since ancient times. It is
distinguished by its material, shape
and the presence of a brim.
As demonstrated by these numerous
hats, headgear is an object that represents a lot.
For some, sentimental value, but these hats
can also represent the country, the clan, an
origin, a religion. And this hat
is also timeless, known in the past for its practicality
and in the future for its style.
