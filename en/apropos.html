Glossary
Fordism:
Industrial development mode aiming to increase productivity by reorganizing labor.
Virtuosity:
Great artistic or technical skill of a person.
Alienation:
The act of giving up or losing.
Vagabond:
One who leads a wandering life.
Totalitarianism:
Totalitarianism is a political regime and system in which there exists a single party, which does not allow any organized opposition, and where the state tends to exercise control over all societal activities. Such a system restricts individual opposition to the state.
Protagonist:
Person who plays the leading role in a matter.
